Advanced Water Purification & Enhancement

The HydroLoop is a multi-stage water treatment system centered around the patented HydroCav device. It utilizes HydroDynamic Cavitation to purify and enhance water, offering a state-of-the-art solution for the Recreational Chlorine spectrum.

Key Benefits & Advantages

Significant Cost Savings

Experience drastically reduced toxic chemical usage and lower labor time and requirements.

Enhanced Efficiency

Provides numerous operational benefits and ensures smooth, uninterrupted water treatment.

Failsafe Protection

Protects against unforeseen failures and human error, reducing liability and preserving your brand.

Unmatched Durability

Constructed from marine-grade materials like copper/nickel, brass, and stainless steel for extreme quality.

Simplified Maintenance

Features a lightweight two-part design with simplistic procedures, adaptable to any setup.

Versatile Design & Sizing

The HydroLoop is engineered for ultimate flexibility, ensuring a perfect fit for any new or existing plumbing configuration.

1.5-inch Model

Advanced System Features

Advanced Monitoring & Safety

  • ▶
    Clear View for Instant Feedback: A custom clear PVC elbow provides a view into the unit, allowing for instant feedback on debris without depressurizing the system.
  • ▶
    Unique Dual Pressure Gauge: Constantly monitors optimal PSI on both high-pressure and low-pressure sides, ensuring an efficient 3-to-1 pressure differential.
  • ▶
    GPM Flow Meter: Assists commercial facilities in complying with government regulations requiring a minimum water turnover flow rate.

Streamlined Maintenance & Backflushing

  • ▶
    Quick Flush Method: A simple turn of a valve removes small debris, ensuring smooth and uninterrupted operation.
  • ▶
    Power Flush Method: A more aggressive method that completely flushes all debris without prolonged downtime.
  • ▶
    Pro Tip: The shipping bucket doubles as a collection bucket for the Power Flush.

Effortless Installation & Bypass

  • ▶
    "One Cut Connection": A perfected, simple two-part process for fast installation, attaching directly to the pump's discharge side.
  • ▶
    Clear Bypass Line: Divert water away from the cavitation zone when vacuuming, preventing clogs by sending bulky debris directly to the filter.
  • ▶
    Uninterrupted Maintenance: Engage the bypass to allow full circulation and filtration while the top half of the unit is removed for service.

Unique Commercial Integration

  • ▶
    Dedicated Accessory Port: A unique female threaded port allows for the quick connection of a commercial water quality monitoring and chemical distribution system.
  • ▶
    No Modification Needed: This feature provides a secure location to connect existing systems without drilling, tapping, or modifying your current PVC setup.
